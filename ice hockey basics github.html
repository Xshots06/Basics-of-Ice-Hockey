The player positions.



In Ice hockey player positions are broken up into four diffrent catagories such as Goalie,Wingers,Defence,and a Center







Goaile


The goalies primary job is to stop the puck from going in to their own net.





Wingers


A team at full strength has two wingers one on the right side of the ice and the oter winger on the left side of the ice. the wingers are supose to stay on the aposing teams defence and also break the buck out of their own zone and into the oposing teams zone by passing and dangling through the other teams players. the wingers are also supose to take shots at the other teams net and try to score.





Defence


A team at full streingth has two defence players on the ice. Their is one defence player on the left half of the ice and the other defense playe is on the right side of the ice. The defences job is to stop the apposing team from breaking out of their zone and into our zone.





Center


This position requires a lot of creativity and a very high level of skill. the center be good at face-offs and passing to their teamates. the center also should be a really good skater and have a power full shot.
